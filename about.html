#OWNYOURSTYLE

We are a local brand born from a desire to create high quality oversized t-shirts that combine comfort, style, and creativity.

OUR BACKGROUND

LIVORD was founded in 2024 with a vision to redefine modern fashion by blending luxury, comfort, and exclusivity. We create high-quality, stylish oversized garments that offer versatility for the modern lifestyle. Each piece is designed to transition effortlessly from day to night, ensuring both style and comfort.

Rooted in slow fashion principles, we focus on craftsmanship and uniqueness by releasing an exclusive design each month. This approach allows us to prioritize quality over quantity, offering our customers a truly special piece. At LIVORD, fashion is more than just clothing. It’s a movement toward self-expression, sustainability, and timeless style.

WHY STRIPE?

Stripes symbolize movement and progression, creating the balance between order and flow. Stripes create a visual rhythm that speaks of strength and harmony, making them the perfect expression of individuality and purpose. Each line represents a path, a choice, or a story.

WHY BEAR?

The bear represents strength, courage, and resilience. Like a bear, our oversized tees are designed to empower you, wrapping you in comfort and bold confidence. More than just clothing, they serve as a reminder to embrace your inner power and carry your story with pride.
